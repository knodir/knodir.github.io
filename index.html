Welcome to my corner of the Internet. I am a PhD student at Computer Science department of University of British Columbia.

I am broadly interested in Systems research. Recently, I have been focusing on datacenter resource scheduling challenges, in particular for networked cloud applications. I have also done some work on Virtual Network Functions. I am advised by Ivan Beschastnikh and Alan Hu at Systopia.
