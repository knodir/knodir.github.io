Welcome to my corner of the Internet. I am a PhD student at Computer Science department of University of British Columbia.

I am broadly interested in Systems research. I focus on resource allocation and networking in data center. Recently, I have been working on challenges of running Network Functions at data center scale. I am advised by Ivan Beschastnikh in the Networks, Systems, and Security laboratory.
