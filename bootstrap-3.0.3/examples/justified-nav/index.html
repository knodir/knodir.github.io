I joined PhD-track of Computer Science department at University of British Colimbia, Vancouver, Canada in 2013 September.

My research interests fall broadly on systems. Recently I have been working on file systems, distributed systems and networking stack.

Education

I got my Master of Science at Embedded Computing Laboratory of Konkuk University, from September 2008 to August 2010 in Seoul, South Korea under supervision of Professor Kim Doo-Hyun.

I got Bachelors degree at Information Technology department of Tashkent University of Information Technologies, from 2004 September to 2008 June in Tashkent, Uzbekistan.

Work experience

Before joining UBC, I was at Electronics and Telecommunications Research Institute (ETRI, Daejeon, South Korea) from 2010 September to 2012 July. There, I worked on several fun stuff such as Cloud resource management, L2 protocol development for Data Center network equipment and etc.

Otherwise

I enjoy reading, in particular inspirational stories, novels. I like running, playing soccer and like everyone else - travelling.
